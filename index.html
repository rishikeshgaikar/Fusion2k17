Theme

One of the great minds to walk on this earth -- Charles Darwin -- gave us the concept of Evolution and Natural Selection. Technology has gone through the same steps although artificially, not naturally. But nonetheless technology has come very far to give us some of the most amazing things in use. From Vaccum Tubes to Transistors; from manual transmission cars to computerised self-driving cars; from basic number crunching computers to Artificial Intelligence. We the students of Padmabhushan Vasantdada Patil Pratishthan's College Of Engineering want to appreciate, engourage and inspire the current generation of Engineers to contribute to the same. One of the main reason we are hoisting the Evolution theme in Fusion 2k17 is to find and embrace the talent amongst the future generation of Engineers. We believe in working towards the greater good of humanity by appreciating the work and sacrifices made by some of the great minds of the past. Be a part of this movement by participating in the events held on the 1st, 2 nd and 3rd of March.
